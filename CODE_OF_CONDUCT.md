# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want to foster an open and welcoming environment.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Gracefully accepting feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling, doxxing, or threats

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please open an issue or contact the maintainer.
